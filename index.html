🔍 What We Do

Regime Alpha is an advanced analytics platform that detects hidden structural shifts in financial markets. Using cutting-edge statistical models and machine learning techniques, we identify when markets change behavior — known as market regimes — and explain why these changes occur.

📈 Why Regimes Matter

Markets are not static — they evolve through periods of growth, volatility, crisis, and recovery. Traditional strategies assume constant risk and return, but this is far from reality. Market regimes affect everything: asset performance, correlations, volatility, liquidity, and macro sensitivity.

Knowing the current regime — and predicting shifts — is essential for:

  • 📊 Adapting trading strategies
  • 💼 Portfolio optimization and rebalancing
  • 🛡️ Managing risk exposure
  • 📉 Anticipating drawdowns and volatility spikes

🧠 What Makes Regime Alpha Unique

Regime Alpha goes beyond price-based clustering. We integrate macroeconomic variables, PCA for dimensionality reduction, and LLM-powered explanations so you not only detect shifts — you understand them.

Our models include:

  • Gaussian Mixture Models (GMM)
  • Bayesian GMM
  • Hidden Markov Models (HMM)
  • Kalman Filter Smoothing
