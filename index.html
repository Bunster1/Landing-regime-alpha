Quantitative Modelling & Math

Our platform leverages Bayesian mixture models, sticky Hidden Markov Models and dimensionality reduction to extract latent market states and forecast regime shifts.

Bayesian GMM

Dirichlet mixture models automatically determine the number of regimes and infer posterior uncertainty over means, covariances and mixing weights.

Sticky HMM

Self‑transition bias captures realistic regime durations and reduces noise. Persistent latent states drive dynamic portfolio allocations.

PCA & Factor Analysis

Dimensionality reduction uncovers dominant risk drivers. Principal components capture macro risk factors and feed our predictive models.

Cluster Visualisation

Visualise portfolio exposures in reduced dimensions to identify diversification and concentration across regimes.
