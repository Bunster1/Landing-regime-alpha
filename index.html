üß† Regime Models Overview

Choose from powerful statistical engines designed to capture nonstationarity and structural evolution across assets:

  • GMM (Gaussian Mixture Model): Unsupervised clustering of return patterns. Simple and fast.
  • Bayesian GMM: Adds uncertainty estimates and guards against overfitting. Ideal for research workflows.
  • HMM (Hidden Markov Model): Introduces temporal persistence and transition logic. Best for regime switching alpha models.
  • Hybrid HMM (Coming Soon): Separates state prediction (via implied data like futures) from state classification (realized market + macro). GPU accelerated. Designed for forward-guided macro environments.

All models are GPU-ready and compatible with backtesting logic, dashboard overlays, and co-pilot interpretation.
